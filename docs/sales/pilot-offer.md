### What the team gets (fixed scope)

1. **Kickoff call (60 min)** — map the target system; agree on the 3 most
   business-critical failure scenarios.
2. **Topology modeling** — we build the dependency model from your architecture
   (no agents, no production access required).
3. **3 failure-scenario simulations** — cascade + N-layer availability analysis
   on the modeled topology.
4. **DORA-aligned evidence pack (PDF)** — scenario assumptions, failure impact,
   control mapping, and remediation notes your engineering / risk / compliance
   teams can review ahead of audit.
5. **Gap report + readout call (45 min)** — top resilience gaps ranked by impact,
   with concrete remediation steps.

### What it is / isn't

- It **is** decision-support: structured evidence for *internal* DORA pre-audit
  review and design-time resilience planning.
- It is **not** legal advice, an audit opinion, or regulatory certification —
  validate outputs with your own engineering, risk, and compliance teams before
  any audit submission. (Same disclosure as the product.)
