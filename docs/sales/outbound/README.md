# DORA Resilience Evidence Sprint — Execution-Ready Outbound Kit

A ready-to-send outbound kit for selling the **DORA Resilience Evidence Sprint**
(fixed scope, 5 business days, **$2,500**, 50% upfront, sanitized data only, no
production access, no PII). It turns the strategic
[outbound campaign](../outbound-campaign.md) into something a solo founder can
run this week.

## Contents

1. **[Part 1 — Target Account Framework](01-target-account-framework.md)** —
   the 60-account framework across three segments (~35 EU payment/e-money
   institutions, ~15 MiCA CASPs, ~10 EU DORA advisory boutiques): ICP criteria,
   where to source the real list (EBA / national NCA registers, MiCA CASP
   registers, Crunchbase, LinkedIn Sales Navigator filter strings), illustrative
   real example companies, and buyer personas.
2. **[Part 2 — Sequences, Cadence & Tracking](02-sequences-cadence-tracking.md)**
   — a 4-touch email sequence per segment, the day-by-day cadence + email/LinkedIn
   channel mix, a tracking-sheet / lightweight CRM column template, objection
   handling, and the 20-minute scoping-call script.

## Founder execution checklist (not done for you)

This kit is a framework and copy library. Before sending, the founder must:

- **Identify and verify the final 60 accounts** — confirm each firm's current
  DORA scope, the right contact, and outreach appropriateness. The example
  companies in Part 1 are **illustrative ICP anchors only** — not customers,
  endorsements, or a contact list.
- **Comply with GDPR / ePrivacy** — use a legitimate-interest B2B basis, no
  purchased lists, and a clear opt-out / unsubscribe in every email. Honor
  opt-outs and keep only the minimal contact data you need.
- **Personalize** every touch (the sequences use `{curly_brace}` placeholders).
