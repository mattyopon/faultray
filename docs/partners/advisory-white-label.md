# FaultRay Advisory Partner Program — White-Label DORA Evidence Engine

## Why partner with FaultRay

EU financial institutions already trust specialist DORA, ICT-risk, and operational-resilience boutiques to guide them through readiness work. Those boutiques own the client relationship, regulatory context, and deal flow — but many do not have a repeatable technical simulation engine for producing defensible resilience evidence.

FaultRay sits behind the advisory firm as a white-label technical evidence engine. The partner leads the client engagement; FaultRay helps generate the dependency analysis, failure-scenario results, DORA article mapping, and remediation backlog that turn advisory work into a productized pre-audit deliverable.

The flagship offer is the **DORA Resilience Evidence Sprint**: fixed scope, **5 business days**, **$2,500**, **50% upfront**, for **one critical service**. It uses **sanitized data only**, requires **no production access**, and processes **no PII**.

FaultRay is intentionally narrow: it supports DORA pre-audit preparation and resilience decision-support. It is **not legal advice**, **not a DORA certification**, **not a compliance guarantee**, and it does **not** replace TLPT, auditor sign-off, or regulatory review.

### Per-service DORA evidence pack structure

Each Evidence Sprint produces a pack for one critical service, typically including:

1. **Scope, assumptions, and input summary**
   - Critical service in scope.
   - Sanitized inputs used.
   - Known exclusions and confidence limits.

2. **Dependency / blast-radius map**
   - Internal systems, data stores, integrations, cloud/SaaS dependencies, and third parties.
   - Service-impact relationships and dependency concentration.

3. **Scenario results**
   - Simulated disruption scenarios such as third-party outage, infrastructure degradation, integration failure, or recovery constraint.
   - Impact narrative, assumptions, evidence gaps, and resilience observations.

4. **SPOF and third-party concentration analysis**
   - Single points of failure.
   - Concentrated dependency risk across vendors, platforms, locations, or operational teams.

5. **DORA article mapping**
   - Evidence mapped to relevant DORA pre-audit themes, including **Articles 11, 12, 24, 25, 28, and 30**.

6. **Remediation backlog**
   - Prioritized technical and governance actions.
   - Suggested ownership, severity, next steps, and retest considerations.

Every pack carries the same honest disclaimer: **FaultRay provides decision-support and DORA pre-audit preparation only. It is not legal advice, not a DORA certification, not a compliance guarantee, and does not replace TLPT, auditor sign-off, regulatory review, or the financial institution’s own obligations.**

### Ready-to-use partner FAQ

**Q1: What data does FaultRay need?**  
FaultRay works from sanitized architecture diagrams, dependency lists, service descriptions, third-party/vendor information, resilience assumptions, and recovery information. It does **not** require production access, credentials, live integrations, or PII.

**Q2: Who delivers the engagement to the financial institution?**  
The partner does. The partner owns the client relationship, workshops, advisory framing, and final readout. FaultRay provides the technical simulation and evidence-pack engine behind the scenes.

**Q3: How does white-labeling work?**  
Evidence packs can carry the partner’s logo, colours, firm name, report styling, and contact details. FaultRay can be unbranded or co-branded depending on the partner agreement.

**Q4: How should partners price this to clients?**  
Partners set their own client pricing. The direct FaultRay Evidence Sprint reference price is $2,500 for one critical service, but partners may bundle the output into larger DORA readiness, operational-resilience, ICT-risk, or third-party-risk engagements.

**Q5: What support is available during a live Sprint?**  
Partners receive priority support during active engagements. Specific channels, response targets, and escalation paths are agreed in the partner agreement.

**Q6: Can one Sprint cover multiple critical services?**  
No. The standard Sprint covers **one critical service**. Multiple services can be handled through additional Sprints or a larger scoped engagement.

**Q7: Does FaultRay perform TLPT, penetration testing, or live chaos testing?**  
No. FaultRay is a pre-audit infrastructure resilience simulator and evidence engine. It does not perform TLPT, penetration testing, legal certification, or live production disruption testing.

**Q8: What does FaultRay not do?**  
FaultRay does not provide legal advice, DORA certification, compliance guarantees, auditor sign-off, regulatory approval, TLPT replacement, or processing of PII.
